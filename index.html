OUR STORY

A Legacy of Excellence

For over a decade, we've been Udaipur's premier vintage car rental service, making royal moments unforgettable

Preserving Automotive Heritage

Founded in 2014 with a passion for classic automobiles, Vintage Car Rental Udaipur has grown from a single vintage car to Rajasthan's most prestigious collection of heritage vehicles.

Each vehicle in our fleet is meticulously restored and maintained to museum-quality standards. We believe every milestone deserves to be celebrated with the timeless elegance only a vintage car can provide.

Over 10 years, we've been part of 500+ celebrations - from intimate palace weddings to grand destination events, Bollywood productions to international music videos.
