OUR STORY

A Decade of
Royal Memories

Founded in 2014 with a passion for preserving automotive heritage, Vintage Car Rental Udaipur has grown from a single classic car to Rajasthan's premier vintage automobile collection.

Our fleet includes rare classics from the golden era of motoring - each vehicle meticulously restored and maintained to museum-quality standards. We believe every milestone deserves to be celebrated in style, with the timeless elegance only a vintage car can provide.

Over 10 years, we've had the privilege of being part of more than 500 celebrations - from intimate palace weddings to grand destination events, from Bollywood film shoots to international music videos. Each journey adds another chapter to our story.
